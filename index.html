Daily Health Tips

Small habits that make a massive difference to your long-term health.

01
💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink 8–10 glasses of water daily. Dehydration causes fatigue, headaches, and poor concentration.

02
🛌

Prioritize Sleep

7–9 hours of sleep per night strengthens immunity, improves mood, and enhances memory.

03
🥗

Balanced Diet

Eat a rainbow — fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats every day.

04
🚶

Daily Movement

Just 30 minutes of walking daily reduces risk of heart disease, diabetes, and depression by 30%.

05
🧼

Hand Hygiene

Wash hands for 20 seconds before meals. This single habit prevents 80% of common infections.

06
🧘

Mental Health

Practice deep breathing, journaling, or meditation. Stress management is as important as physical health.
